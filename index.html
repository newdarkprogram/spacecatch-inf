Faq

What is SpaceCatch?

SpaceCatch is a free to play and play to earn augmented reality-based game with RPG features. More about the game

Who is behind the development of SpaceCatch?

SpaceCatch was developed by a diverse team of experienced personalities from the world of cryptocurrencies and games themselves. You can see for yourself in the section. More about our team

What is augmented reality?

Augmented reality (AR) games connect the real world with the virtual one. They use a camera and GPS to incorporate real-world elements into gameplay.

Is the game available worldwide?

SpaceCatch is an online game and therefore the game is available wherever there is a stable internet connection.

What devices is the game available on?

SpaceCatch is available on iOS and Android devices free of charge.

What is NFT?

NFTs (non-fungible tokens) are unique cryptographic tokens that exist on a blockchain and cannot be replicated. These tokens can represent various in-game items. More about SpaceCatch NFTs

What will NFT be used for in SpaceCatch?

In our game, you will be able to earn NFT boxes just by playing. These boxes will contain various NFT items and materials, which will help you to earn more rewards and play more effectively. More about NFT

What is a $CATCH token?

Our $CATCH token is ERC-20 standard, based on Arbitrum. More about the Token

What is the $CATCH token used for?

With the $CATCH token you can buy NFT packs, NFT items, boosters, raffles, invasion passes and much more in our marketplace. Get to know about the $CATCH utility.

Can I stake the $CATCH token?

Yes, the $CATCH token can be staked. All details are specified in  tokenomics.
