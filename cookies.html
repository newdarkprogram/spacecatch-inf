2.1 Cookies are short text files that a website sends to your browser. Cookies are important because without them, browsing the Internet would be much more difficult. Cookies allow us to make better use of our website and to tailor its content to your needs; almost every website in the world uses them. Cookies are useful because they increase the user-friendliness of a repeatedly visited website.

2.2 The website may use the following types of cookies:

2.2.1 Session (i.e. temporary) cookies allow us to link your individual activities while you are browsing this website.
